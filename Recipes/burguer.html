How to make Burguers


A delicious burguer to kick your hunger out the window!

This burger is the ultimate bite—juicy, flavorful, and perfectly seasoned for that classic, mouthwatering taste. With a deliciously seared crust and tender, juicy center, it’s everything a great burger should be.

The best part? You can make it right at home! I’ll show you how to craft the perfect patty and build a burger that’s truly unforgettable.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound ground beef (80% lean)
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 4 hamburger buns
  • Cheese slices (optional)
  • Lettuce, tomato, pickles, and other toppings (optional)

And after you gather all the ingredients, the last thing is to just follow these steps.

Steps:

  1. Preheat a grill or skillet over medium-high heat.
  2. In a bowl, combine ground beef, garlic powder, onion powder, black pepper, salt, and Worcestershire sauce. Mix gently until just combined.
  3. Divide the mixture into 4 equal portions and shape each into a patty, making a slight indentation in the center of each.
  4. Grill or cook the patties for about 4-5 minutes per side, or until they reach your desired level of doneness.
  5. If adding cheese, place a slice on each patty during the last minute of cooking to melt.
  6. Toast the hamburger buns lightly on the grill or in a skillet.
  7. Assemble the burger by placing the patty on the bottom bun, then adding any desired toppings like lettuce, tomato, and pickles.
  8. Top with the other half of the bun, and enjoy!
