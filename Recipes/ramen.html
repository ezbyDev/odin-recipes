How to make Ramen


A delicious ramen soup you can make in just 10 minutes!

This homemade ramen is warm, comforting, and packed with flavor—perfect for a quick and satisfying meal. With savory broth, tender noodles, and a touch of soy and sesame, it’s a simple yet delicious take on classic ramen.

The best part? You can make it in minutes! I’ll show you how to whip up this easy, flavorful bowl right at home.

Ingredients:

  • 3 cups water
  • 1 package ramen noodles (with seasoning packet)
  • 1 tsp soy sauce
  • ½ tsp sesame oil
  • Chopped green onions (optional, for garnish)

And after you gather all the ingredients, the last thing is to just follow these steps.

Steps:

  1. In a saucepan, bring 3 cups of water to a boil.
  2. Add the ramen noodles and cook for about 3 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  3. Stir in the seasoning packet, soy sauce, and sesame oil.
  4. Reduce heat and let simmer for 1-2 minutes.
  5. Pour into a bowl and garnish with green onions or other toppings if desired.
  6. Serve hot and enjoy!
